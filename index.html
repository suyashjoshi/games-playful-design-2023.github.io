About

Welcome to TIGHTROPE, a showcase of work by the 2022 MA Independent Games & Playable Experience Design cohort at Goldsmiths, University of London.

TIGHTROPE interprets tension in play, from curiosity and uncertainty to challenge and courage, across the spectrum of play between rules and make-believe.

Through puzzle games, interactive fiction, Kinect & VR experiences, and role play, we explore what it means to walk metaphorical tightropes - noticing, feeling, learning, trusting, wandering, being.

In this weekend of play, we invite you to walk with us, to leave the door open for the unknown, to see the value of tension in creating new conversations.
